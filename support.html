Account & Registration

  • How do I create an account? — Download the Beauty Vision app, tap "Register", and follow the steps. You'll need your name, phone number, and a password.
  • I didn't receive a verification code. — Check your spam/junk folder (for email) or wait a moment and tap "Resend". Make sure you entered the correct phone number or email.
  • How do I reset my password? — On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions sent to your email or phone.
  • How do I delete my account? — Go to Settings in the app and select "Delete Account". Your personal data will be removed within 30 days.

Loyalty & Bonus Points

  • How do I earn bonus points? — Show your in-app QR code at any Beauty Vision physical store when making a purchase. Points are credited automatically based on the purchase amount.
  • What are the earning rates? — 2% (₾100–300), 3% (₾300–700), 4% (₾700–1,000), 5% (₾1,000+) on physical store purchases.
  • Where can I see my points balance? — Open the app and go to the Wallet tab to view your current balance and transaction history.

Orders & Payments

  • What payment methods are accepted? — We accept card payments through Keepz.
  • Where can I view my order history? — Go to your profile in the app to see all past orders and their statuses.

Privacy & Data

  • How is my data protected? — We use encryption, secure password hashing, and strict access controls. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
  • How do I exercise my data rights? — Contact us at sh.chachanidze@beautyvision.ge to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
