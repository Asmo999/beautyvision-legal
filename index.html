3. How We Use Your Data

  • Account management — to create and maintain your account, verify your identity via email or SMS
  • Loyalty program — to calculate and credit bonus points and track tier status
  • Customer support — to respond to your inquiries
  • Marketing — to send promotional emails (only if you opt in during registration)
  • App improvement — to analyze usage patterns and fix bugs

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Law of Georgia on Personal Data Protection:

  • Contract performance (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) — processing necessary to fulfill your orders and manage your account
  • Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) — for marketing communications and optional birthday data; you may withdraw consent at any time
  • Legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) — for fraud prevention, app security, and service improvement
  • Legal obligation (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c)) — when required by Georgian tax or commercial law

5. Data Sharing

We share your personal data only with the following third parties, strictly for the purposes described:

  • SMS provider — delivery of verification codes to Georgian phone numbers (receives phone number and code only)
  • Email service provider — delivery of verification emails and marketing emails (if opted in)
  • Hosting provider — servers where the App's backend runs (data encrypted at rest and in transit)

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We do not transfer data outside of Georgia and the European Economic Area without appropriate safeguards.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts — data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts — personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion; anonymized transaction records may be retained for accounting/tax purposes for up to 6 years as required by Georgian law
  • Verification codes — automatically expire and are deleted after 10 minutes
  • Marketing consent records — retained for the duration of your account plus 1 year after deletion for legal compliance

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Georgian data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate personal data (you can do this directly in the App under Personal Information)
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and personal data (available in App settings)
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we process your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw marketing consent at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sh.chachanidze@beautyvision.ge. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Passwords stored using bcrypt hashing (never in plain text)
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or via email. The "Effective date" at the top of this document indicates when it was last revised.
